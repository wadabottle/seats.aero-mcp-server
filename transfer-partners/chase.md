# Chase Ultimate Rewards Airline Transfer Partners

> Last updated: 2026-06-01

## Overview

Chase Ultimate Rewards is one of the most flexible transferable points currencies in the US market. Points earned on eligible Chase cards (Sapphire Preferred, Sapphire Reserve, Ink Business Preferred, etc.) can be transferred to 10 airline loyalty programs and 4 hotel programs. All airline transfers occur at a 1:1 ratio — one Chase point becomes one airline mile — with no transfer fees. The minimum transfer is 1,000 points, and transfers must be made in increments of 1,000. Chase periodically offers transfer bonuses (typically 20–30% extra miles) to select partners, so it is worth checking for active bonuses before transferring. Emirates Skywards was removed as a Chase transfer partner on October 16, 2025 and is no longer available.

## Notes

**Transfer minimums and increments:** All partners require a minimum transfer of 1,000 points, in 1,000-point increments.

**Fees:** Chase charges no transfer fees to any partner.

**Transfer bonuses:** Chase runs periodic transfer bonuses (typically 20–30%) to individual partners. These are time-limited and partner-specific. Check the Chase Ultimate Rewards portal or points/miles blogs for active promotions before transferring.

**Avios pooling:** Aer Lingus AerClub, British Airways Executive Club, and Iberia Plus all use the Avios currency. Points transferred to any of these programs can be pooled into a single Avios balance via the British Airways or Iberia app, allowing you to combine balances earned from multiple Chase transfers for a single high-value redemption.

**Emirates removal:** Emirates Skywards was discontinued as a Chase transfer partner effective October 16, 2025.

**Singapore KrisFlyer delay:** KrisFlyer is the only airline partner where transfers are not instant — allow 24–48 hours before booking an award that depends on transferred miles.

**Irreversibility:** All transfers are one-way and permanent. Points cannot be transferred back to Chase once sent to a partner program. Confirm award availability before initiating a transfer.

**Eligible Chase cards:** Transfer partners are available on Chase Sapphire Preferred, Chase Sapphire Reserve, Ink Business Preferred, and when points are combined from other Chase cards (Freedom, Freedom Flex, Freedom Unlimited) into a Sapphire or Ink Preferred account.

# Capital One Airline Transfer Partners

> Last updated: 2026-06-01

## Overview

Capital One Venture, Venture X, VentureOne, and Spark Miles cardholders can transfer miles to 18 airline loyalty programs and 4 hotel programs. The majority of airline partners transfer at a 1:1 ratio with no transfer fees. A handful of partners (Emirates, EVA Air, Japan Airlines, JetBlue) carry less favorable ratios. Most transfers are near-instant; a few partners take up to 36 hours. Capital One periodically runs limited-time transfer bonuses (e.g., a 20% bonus to Qantas that ran through May 31, 2026). There are no transfer fees charged by Capital One.

**Minimum transfer:** 1,000 miles per transaction.

## Notes

### Transfer Ratios Explained
- **1:1** — 1,000 Capital One miles = 1,000 partner miles/points (full value)
- **2:1.5 (4:3)** — 1,000 Capital One miles = 750 partner miles (25% loss; applies to Emirates, EVA Air, JAL)
- **5:3** — 1,000 Capital One miles = 600 TrueBlue points (40% loss; applies to JetBlue only)

### Transfer Fees
Capital One charges **no transfer fees**. Individual loyalty programs may charge their own fees for award changes or cancellations.

### Transfer Bonuses
Capital One runs periodic bonus promotions. Recent examples:
- **Qantas Frequent Flyer** — 20% bonus (ran through May 31, 2026; expired)
- **Japan Airlines Mileage Bank** — 30% bonus (ran through April 30, 2026; expired)

Check Capital One's transfer page or [frequentmiler.com/current-point-transfer-bonuses/](https://frequentmiler.com/current-point-transfer-bonuses/) for live promotions.

### Eligible Cards
Transfers are available from: Venture X, Venture X Business, Venture, Venture One, Spark Miles, and Spark Miles Select.

### Program Changes
Capital One added **Japan Airlines Mileage Bank**, **Qatar Airways Privilege Club**, and **I Prefer Hotel Rewards** on September 23, 2025.

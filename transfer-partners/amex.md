# American Express Membership Rewards Airline Transfer Partners

> Last updated: 2026-06-01

## Overview

American Express Membership Rewards (MR) is one of the most flexible transferable points currencies, with **17 airline transfer partners** (16 active after Etihad Guest ends June 30, 2026) covering major alliances and regions worldwide. The standard transfer ratio is 1:1, though a few partners offer better or worse ratios. Most transfers are instant or same-day; a handful take 2–5 days. U.S.-based airline programs (Delta and JetBlue) carry a small excise tax fee; all international programs transfer free of charge. Transfers are always one-way and irreversible — confirm award availability before transferring.

**Note:** Hawaiian Airlines (HawaiianMiles) ended its Amex transfer partnership on June 30, 2025 following Alaska Airlines' acquisition. Etihad Guest ends June 30, 2026.

## Notes

### Fees
- **Delta SkyMiles and JetBlue TrueBlue** are the only airline partners with a transfer fee: **$0.06 per 1,000 points transferred**, capped at **$99 per transaction**. This offsets a U.S. federal excise tax Amex must pay.
- All other airline partners transfer **free of charge**.
- To avoid the Delta fee, consider transferring to Virgin Atlantic Flying Club and booking Delta-operated flights via Flying Club partner awards.

### Transfer Minimums
- Most partners: **1,000 MR minimum**
- JetBlue TrueBlue: **250 MR minimum**
- Qantas Frequent Flyer: **500 MR minimum**

### Transfer Bonuses
- Amex periodically runs **transfer bonus promotions** (typically 20–40%) to select partners. These are time-limited and not guaranteed. Check the Amex portal or Points Guy/Upgraded Points for current bonuses before transferring.

### Ending Partnerships
- **Hawaiian Airlines (HawaiianMiles)**: Partnership ended June 30, 2025 following Alaska Airlines acquisition.
- **Etihad Guest**: Partnership ends **June 30, 2026**. Transfer any remaining points before this deadline.

### Avios Ecosystem
Aer Lingus AerClub, British Airways Executive Club, Iberia Plus, and Qatar Airways Privilege Club all use Avios as their currency and can be pooled/transferred between programs via the Avios website, giving added flexibility once you've transferred from Amex.

### Strategy Tips
- **Never transfer speculatively.** All transfers are one-way and irreversible. Confirm award availability in the partner program first.
- **International partners are fee-free.** For Delta flights, Virgin Atlantic Flying Club is often a better recipient than Delta SkyMiles directly (no fee, and sometimes better redemption rates).
- **ANA and Aeromexico** have the slowest transfer times — plan several days ahead for time-sensitive bookings.
- **Singapore KrisFlyer** and **Virgin Atlantic Flying Club** are consistently rated among the highest-value transfer destinations for premium cabin redemptions.

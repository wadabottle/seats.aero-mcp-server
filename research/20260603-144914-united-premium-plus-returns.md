## Verdict: Is United Premium Plus Viable for the Return?

**Yes — with strong caveats on gateway and window.**

### What the data confirms

1. **United Premium Plus IS available on LHR→SFO** in both windows (contradicts the prior zero-result direct search — bulk data confirms real availability). The route shows direct UA metal, cabin W.

2. **Window B unlocks saver pricing**: LHR→SFO at 50,000 miles/pax on Nov 10, 11, 12 is exactly the nominal saver rate. This is the clearest "textbook" award in the dataset.

3. **FRA→SFO** at 94,900–95,100 miles (Window B) with only $167–173 in taxes is a compelling alternative if the trip includes Germany or a Frankfurt connection — total cost of taxes is 60% lower than LHR.

4. **DUB, MUC, ZRH, AMS** all show 100k-mile options with very low taxes ($49–$147), though most are indirect routing. These are dynamic-priced (not saver) and require positioning to those gateways.
